Coverage-driven RTL analysis task: close functional coverage for the protected
`top_dec` source in `rtl/top_dec.v`.

Rules:
- You may edit only `tests/test_sequences.py`.
- Do not modify the protected RTL, coverage model, harness, or support library.
- Do not call coverage APIs directly from the editable file. Coverage must come
  from observed RTL evidence through the provided helper functions.

Goal:
- Reach at least 90% functional coverage within 4 iterations.
- Each iteration replays the same analysis twice. Flaky or nondeterministic
  tests are penalized.

Expected analysis topics:
- Three pipeline stages.
- Input valid-gated pipeline registers and zero-muxing.
- Route computation and permutation network.
- Stage-2 local/bypass registration, request vector, valid vector, allocator.
- Local ejection/injection block and 5-port crossbar.
- PV bypass forwarding priority and bit positions.
- Stage-3 output registers and output muxing.
